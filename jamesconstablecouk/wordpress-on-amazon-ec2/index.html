WordPress on Amazon EC2

With a wee bit of time to spare over the Christmas period we took the opportunity to move our hosting infrastructure from an aging Fasthosts dedicated server to an Amazon EC2 instance. All of our client sites are now up and running, as is my blog, which has also been freshly updated to WordPress 2.7. Anyone who’s been here before may have noticed how much faster the site is on EC2 and the same can be said for all of the other sites running on it.

I’ll write more soon about our setup.
